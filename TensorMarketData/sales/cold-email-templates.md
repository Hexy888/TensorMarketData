## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Formulas:
- "Monetize Your [Data Type] Data"
- "[Company] + TensorMarketData"
- "Power Your AI Agents with [Data Type]"
- "Quick Question About [Relevant Goal]"
- "AI Data for [Their Use Case]"

### Timing:
- **Best Days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best Times**: 8-10 AM, 1-3 PM (local time)
- **Follow-Up Cadence**: Day 3, Day 7, Day 14

### Personalization Tips:
- Reference recent company news
- Mention specific data products
- Identify specific team members
- Reference mutual connections
- Reference industry events

### Attachments:
- One-pager for first contact
- Case studies for follow-ups
- API documentation for technical buyers
