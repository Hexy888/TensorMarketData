## Contact Research Template

### For Each Company, Research:
- **LinkedIn**: Find VP of Data, Chief Data Officer, Head of Partnerships
- **Recent News**: Look for data partnership announcements
- **Product Pages**: Check for API/data product offerings
- **Job Postings**: Identify data strategy roles
- **Conference Talks**: Find data leadership speaking slots

### Key Contacts to Target:
1. VP/Head of Data Strategy
2. Chief Data Officer (CDO)
3. Head of Business Development
4. Head of Partnerships
5. Director of Data Monetization
6. Product Manager - Data Products

## Partnership Value Proposition for Data Providers

### Why List on TensorMarketData:
1. **New Revenue Stream**: Monetize existing data assets
2. **AI Agent Buyers**: Access growing market of AI developers
3. **API-First Platform**: Built for programmatic access
4. **Enterprise Compliance**: SOC 2, GDPR, CCPA ready
5. **Usage-Based Pricing**: Maximize data value with pay-per-use
6. **Developer Experience**: Self-service discovery and integration

### Data Types In Demand:
- Company firmographics & hierarchy
- Contact data & professional profiles
- Financial/credit data
- Technographic data
- Intent/engagement signals
- Industry/market research
- Regulatory/legal data
- Supply chain/partner data
