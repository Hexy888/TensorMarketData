Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as an API call?

Each request to /search, /supplier/{id}, or /supplier/{id}/inventory counts as one API call and deducts 1 credit.

Can I carry over unused credits?

Credits reset monthly and do not carry over. Upgrade your plan if you need more capacity.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial with full access. No credit card required to start.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
