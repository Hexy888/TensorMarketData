## 5. Engagement Do's and Don'ts

### ‚úÖ DO:
- Reply quickly (within 1 hour for launch week)
- Be authentic, not corporate
- Engage with critics thoughtfully
- Share technical details generously
- Celebrate user wins publicly
- RT/shout out supportive community members
- Ask questions to understand concerns
- Keep replies short and scannable

### ‚ùå DON'T:
- Be defensive or dismissive
- Use excessive emojis or hype language
- Engage in arguments publicly (move to DM)
- Overpromise on features
- Ignore negative feedback
- Be overly promotional (balance value + pitch)
- Copy/paste the same replies (personalize!)
- Delete negative comments (transparency wins)
