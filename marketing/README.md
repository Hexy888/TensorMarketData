## Usage Notes

### Customization Required
Before using any templates, fill in:
- [Founder Name]
- [Company Name]
- [City]
- [Launch Date]
- [Product Hunt URL]
- [Investor Name]
- [Dates and specific numbers]

### Recommended Launch Timeline
1. **2-3 weeks before**: Post blog content to build awareness
2. **1 week before**: Start Twitter teaser campaign
3. **3 days before**: Send pre-launch email to waitlist
4. **Launch day**: Full push across all channels
5. **Day after**: Follow-up content and community engagement

 Channel Strategy###
- **Twitter**: Daily posts starting 2 weeks before launch
- **LinkedIn**: 1 major article 1 week before, product post on launch day
- **Blog**: Post 2-3 weeks before launch for SEO/value
- **Product Hunt**: Launch day focus with all-hands engagement
- **Email**: 2-3 touchpoints (announce, reminder, launch)
