## FAQ for Launch

### What is TensorMarketData?
TensorMarketData is a B2B data marketplace for AI developers. We offer verified datasets with API-first access and agent-native design.

### How is this different from Hugging Face?
Hugging Face is a model hub with community datasets. TensorMarketData is a curated marketplace with verified quality, API access, and enterprise-grade features.

### How is this different from Scale AI or Appen?
Scale AI and Appen are annotation services. TensorMarketData is a marketplace where anyone can buy and sell datasets.

### What data categories do you support?
We launch with NLP, computer vision, structured data, and domain-specific datasets (healthcare, finance, legal).

### How does verification work?
Every dataset goes through our verification process: accuracy checks, freshness validation, provenance documentation, and compliance review.

### What's your pricing model?
For buyers: Pay per dataset or subscription options.
For sellers: 80% revenue share, transparent pricing.

### Is there a free tier?
Yes! We offer a free tier with limited access for evaluation.

### How do you handle compliance?
We verify licensing and compliance for regulated datasets. HIPAA, GDPR, and other certifications where applicable.

### Can AI agents use this?
Yes! Our entire platform is API-first and agent-native. AI agents can search, purchase, and download datasets autonomously.
