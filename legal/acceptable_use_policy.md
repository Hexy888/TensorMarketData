# Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") establishes acceptable use of TensorMarketData's platform, services, and APIs. By using our Services, you agree to comply with this policy.

## 2. Prohibited Uses

### 2.1 Illegal Activities
You may NOT use our Services to:
- Engage in illegal activities or promote illegal conduct
- Violate any applicable laws, regulations, or ordinances
- Infringe upon third-party intellectual property rights
- Facilitate or participate in fraud, theft, or piracy

### 2.2 Harmful Content
You may NOT:
- Distribute malware, viruses, or other harmful code
- Create content that harasses, defames, or discriminates
- Publish content that is obscene, pornographic, or sexually explicit
- Share content depicting violence or self-harm

### 2.3 Security Violations
You may NOT:
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to our systems or data
- Conduct security testing without authorization
- Bypass or attempt to bypass rate limits
- Use automated tools to scrape or crawl platform data (beyond API usage)
- Introduce malicious code or backdoors
- Interfere with service availability for other users

### 2.4 Abuse and Harassment
You may NOT:
- Harass, threaten, or abuse other users or staff
- Engage in spamming or unsolicited communications
- Impersonate any person or entity
- Create false or misleading accounts
- Engage in activities designed to disrupt or harm the platform

## 3. Data Marketplace Rules

### 3.1 Data Providers
When listing data, you must NOT:
- List data you don't own or lack rights to distribute
- Provide false or misleading data descriptions
- Include personal data without lawful basis
- List prohibited content (see Section 4)
- Manipulate pricing or reviews

### 3.2 Data Buyers
When purchasing data, you must:
- Use data only as licensed
- Not redistribute purchased data beyond license terms
- Not attempt to reverse-engineer data licensing
- Not share access credentials

### 3.3 API Usage
Your API usage must NOT:
- Exceed rate limits or attempt to circumvent them
- Generate unreasonable server load
- Use APIs to build competing products
- Cache data beyond permitted levels

## 4. Prohibited Content

The following content may NOT be listed, sold, or distributed via our platform:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Illegal data | Stolen data, pirated content, illegal APIs |
| Personal data | Unlawfully collected PII, sensitive data without consent |
| Hate content | Content promoting violence or discrimination |
| Explicit content | Child sexual abuse material, non-consensual intimate imagery |
| Dangerous content | Instructions for illegal weapons, dangerous activities |
| Deceptive content | Fake data, synthetic media for fraud |

## 5. Enforcement

### 5.1 Monitoring
We monitor for violations through:
- Automated systems detecting abuse patterns
- User reports and complaints
- Manual review of flagged content
- Security research and threat intelligence

### 5.2 Violation Consequences

| Severity | First Offense | Repeat Offense |
|----------|---------------|----------------|
| Minor | Warning, temporary suspension | Permanent ban |
| Moderate | Temporary suspension (7-30 days) | Permanent ban |
| Severe | Permanent ban | N/A |
| Illegal | Immediate ban, legal referral | N/A |

### 5.3 Enforcement Actions
When violations occur, we may:
- Issue warnings
- Remove or block content
- Suspend or terminate accounts
- Restrict API access
- Report to law enforcement
- Pursue legal remedies

## 6. Reporting Violations

If you observe violations of this policy, report them:

- **Platform:** Use "Report" button on listings
- **Email:** abuse@tensormarketdata.com
- **Emergency:** security@tensormarketdata.com (for security issues)

Include: description, evidence, affected content/accounts.

## 7. Appeal Process

If you believe a moderation decision was made in error:

1. Submit appeal via help center within 14 days
2. Provide detailed explanation and evidence
3. Appeals reviewed within 10 business days
4. Decision is final except for exceptional circumstances

## 8. Policy Changes

We may update this policy as needed. Material changes communicated via:
- Email to registered users
- Platform announcements
- Updated "Last Updated" date

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

## 9. Contact

Questions about this policy: policy@tensormarketdata.com
Abuse reports: abuse@tensormarketdata.com
