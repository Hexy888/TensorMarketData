Frequently asked questions

Which platforms do you support?
We start with Google Business Profile — the highest-impact platform for local businesses. Additional platforms available as needed.
Do you respond to every review?
Yes. Every new review is classified and a response is drafted. No review gets missed.
Can you post replies automatically?
4-5★ can auto-post if you enable it. 1-3★ always require your approval first.
Can you remove bad reviews?
No. We can't remove reviews. We help you respond professionally, which often leads to the reviewer updating their review.
What do you need to start?
Google Business Profile access, brand voice guidelines, and an escalation contact email.
