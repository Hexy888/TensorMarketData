Questions, answered

Everything you need to know about our lead generation service.

What's included in a lead? ▼
A "lead" is 1 verified decision-maker contact record. Each package includes defined fields (name, title, verified email, company details). See Pricing for exact columns.
How are emails verified? ▼
We use a verification process prior to delivery to reduce invalid emails. If an email is invalid within 30 days, we replace it 1:1.
What does the replacement guarantee cover? ▼
30-day 1:1 replacement for invalid emails (hard bounce or failed verification). Replacement covers emails only; it does not guarantee replies, meetings, or revenue.
How fast is delivery? ▼
~48 hours typical. Complex ICPs or large orders may take longer. Rush available.
Can you match strict titles and geographies? ▼
Yes. Pro supports clear title and geo targeting. Premium is designed for strict ICP constraints (C-suite only, niche verticals, tight geos).
What format do you deliver? ▼
CSV/Excel exports with consistent columns, ready for outreach or CRM import.
