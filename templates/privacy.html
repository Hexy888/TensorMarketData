Privacy Policy

This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information for our review management services.

1. Information We Collect

Contact information submitted via forms (email, business name). Review platform credentials (for read-only access). Brand voice guidelines and response templates. Billing information.

2. How We Use Information

To provide review monitoring and response services. To communicate about account status, approvals, and reports. To improve our services.

3. Data We Access

We access review data from connected platforms (Google Business Profile, Yelp, etc.) in read-only mode to monitor and draft responses. We do not share this data with third parties.

4. Client Data

Clients own their review data, templates, and brand guidelines. We retain this data only while the subscription is active.

5. Data Retention

We retain service data while the account is active and for 30 days after cancellation for operational purposes.

6. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect client data. Platform credentials are encrypted.

7. Contact

Questions: support@tensormarketdata.com
