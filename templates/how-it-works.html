Get Started

Systemized reputation ops — with controls.

We monitor, draft, and report. You approve the negatives. Here's exactly how it works.

1

Connect your review platforms

We link to your Google Business Profile (and other platforms as needed). You grant read/write access, and we start pulling in reviews within hours.

What you need: Google Business Profile admin access, additional platform credentials, contact email for notifications.
2

Review inbox + classification

Every new review appears in your dashboard with a classification: Positive (4-5★), Neutral (3★), Negative (1-2★), or High-risk.

3

Draft replies in your brand voice

We draft responses using your approved templates. Each draft matches your brand tone, references specific details, and includes a clear next step.

4

You approve negatives

Positives can auto-post if you enable autopost. Negatives always go to your approval queue first.

Approval options: Approve → Post / Edit → Tweak and post / Reject → Don't post / Escalate → Flag for special handling.
5

Post and audit log

Once approved (or autoposted for positives), we post the response, log the action in your audit trail, and update the review status.

6

Weekly scorecards + monthly insights

Every Monday: total reviews, average rating, response time, coverage, top themes, escalations. Package B/C gets monthly insights.
