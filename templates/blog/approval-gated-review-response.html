The Approval-Gated Review Response System (Template Included)

Every HVAC business owner faces the same problem: you get a review, you need to respond, but you're busy running the business. So responses either don't get written, or they go live without a second thought.

Neither option is good. Unanswered reviews hurt your ranking. Poorly crafted responses hurt your brand.

What is an Approval-Gated System?

Simple: every review gets a drafted response. But only the ones you approve actually go live.

  • 4-5 star reviews: Can auto-post if you enable it
  • 1-3 star reviews: Always require your approval first
  • High-risk content: Flagged and escalated immediately

This gives you control without the time sink.

Why It Works

  1. Consistency: Every review gets a response
  2. Quality control: Nothing goes live without review
  3. Speed: Drafts are generated in minutes, not hours
  4. Audit trail: Every action is logged

Template: Response to Negative Review

Dear {reviewer_name},

Thank you for your feedback. We're sorry to hear your experience wasn't what you expected.

We'd like to make this right. Please contact us directly at {contact_email} so we can address your concerns.

Sincerely,
{signoff}

What About Positive Reviews?

For 5-star reviews, you have options:

  • Auto-post them without approval
  • Review them first, then approve
  • Let our team handle it entirely

You decide. The system adapts to your preferences.

The Bottom Line

Your reputation is worth protecting. An approval-gated system gives you control without consuming your day.
