Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our reputation operations.

Which review platforms do you support?

We start with Google Business Profile — it's the highest-impact platform for local businesses. We can add Yelp, Facebook, TripAdvisor, and other major platforms as needed.

How fast do you respond?

We draft responses within 8 business hours of a new review appearing. That's our SLA. Most reviews are handled same-day.

Do you respond to every review?

Yes. Every new review is classified and a response is drafted. No review gets missed.

Do you post replies automatically?

4-5★ reviews can auto-post if you enable it in settings. 1-3★ reviews always require your approval first.

What requires approval?

1-3★ reviews always need approval. Any review with legal threats, discrimination, safety concerns, or profanity gets escalated immediately.

Can you remove bad reviews?

No. We can't remove reviews. What we do is help you respond professionally, which often leads to the reviewer updating their review.

Do you handle multi-location?

Yes, that's Package C. Each location gets separate dashboards, templates, escalation routing, and reporting.

How does review request automation work?

After a customer completes a purchase, we send a request asking for a review. You provide the customer list, we manage timing and templates. One follow-up max.

Can we customize brand voice?

Absolutely. During onboarding, you provide brand voice guidelines or we use our professional default. You approve all templates.

What reports do we receive?

Weekly: total reviews, average rating, response time, coverage, top themes. Monthly (Package B/C): insights + recommendations.

How do we contact support?

Email only: nova@tensormarketdata.com. Response within 1 business day. We don't offer phone support.

What if we cancel?

Cancel anytime. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No refunds for partial months.
