FAQ

What is included in a lead?

A "lead" is 1 verified decision-maker contact record (not an inbound conversion). Each package includes a defined set of fields (name, title, verified email, and company details). See Pricing for exact columns.

How are emails verified?

We use a verification process prior to delivery to reduce invalid emails. If an email is invalid within 30 days, we replace it 1:1 with evidence (see guarantee).

What does the 30-day replacement guarantee cover?

Invalid email = hard bounce or failed verification evidence. We replace invalid emails 1:1 within 30 days.

Replacement covers emails only; it does not guarantee replies, meetings, or revenue.

How fast is delivery?

Delivery is ~48 hours typical. Complex ICPs or large orders may take longer. Rush available.

Can you match strict job titles and geographies?

Yes. Pro supports clear title and geo targeting. Premium is designed for strict ICP constraints (C-suite only, niche verticals, tight geos, exclusions).

What format do you deliver?

CSV/Excel exports with consistent columns, ready for outreach or CRM import.

Do you honor suppression lists / opt-outs?

Yes. If you provide a suppression list, we will exclude those contacts from delivery. We also honor do-not-contact requests when received.

Do you provide phone numbers?

Only if explicitly included in your order scope. Otherwise, delivery is email-first.

Is this compliant?

We provide B2B contact data and honor suppression/opt-out requests. You are responsible for ensuring your outreach complies with applicable laws and platform policies.
