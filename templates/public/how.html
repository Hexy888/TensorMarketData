How it works

A clean loop from review → reply → record

1) Connect

Connect Google Business Profile and select the correct location.

2) Pull reviews

Ops job pulls new reviews (with pagination) and stores them idempotently.

3) Draft replies

Draft replies are generated using your saved brand voice.

4) Approve + post

1–3★ requires approval. 4–5★ may auto-post if enabled.

Operational guarantees
  • Retries/backoff for transient Google failures
  • Idempotent posting guards (no double-post)
  • Nightly reconcile verifies posted replies exist
