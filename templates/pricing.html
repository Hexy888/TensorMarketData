Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a lead?

1 verified decision-maker contact record = first_name, last_name, title, verified_work_email, company info. See exact columns per package above.

How are emails verified?

We use multi-step verification: syntax check, domain validation, and SMTP verification. Plus our 30-day replacement policy covers any invalid emails.

What does the 30-day replacement guarantee cover?

1:1 replacement for invalid emails (hard bounces). Does NOT guarantee inbox placement, replies, meetings, or revenue. Provide bounce evidence for replacements.
